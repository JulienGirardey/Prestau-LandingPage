The 3 Key Features

Create a mission

Manage your missions

Apply for a mission

A person can apply for a mission they find interesting.

About the project

Prestau is the result of two friend's initiative to simplify access to occasional missions for everyone. Sensitive to the difficulties encountered when searching for flexible jobs, they imagined a modern, intuitive and human platform, allowing everyone to propose or find a mission with ease. Our team then took charge of the application's design and development, in order to bring their vision to life.

This project was completed in March 2026 and is part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
